### Product Idea - Open Table

I have a basic idea for an application that will allow users to find the restaurant of their choice and book a reservation for dining with that particular restaurant. At some point, the user will have to specify how many are in the party and the date of the reservation for the restaurant in question. 


The phases for the product should be as follows:

Phase 1 - The basic ability to sign-in, specify the number of people in the party and reserve an open time for dining from a listing of restaurants

Phase 2 - Enhance on features of v1, with the ability to send out notifications of the booking, along with the ability to create the calendar event for the reservation along with link for directions to the 
selected restaurant. 

Phase 3 - The ability for customers to leave reviews for the restaurants that are enlisted in the product as a helpful promotion. If available, the restaurant can provide a picture of the entrees 
on the selected menu at that time for users to indicate what they had at that particular restaurant for the indicated time. 


Phase 4 - A favorites section that highlights the most booked restaurants for the week or month, or locale.
